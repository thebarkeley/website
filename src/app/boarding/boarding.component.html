The Barkeley Hotel

Going away for a vacation or long-weekend? You can give your dog one, too, and have peace of mind that your dog is under the steady, protective care of our trained staff. We're committed to making The Barkeley your dog's home away from home.

Daycare is included with our boarding service. Our guests will have a full day of play and, come bedtime, your pup will be tired, happy and ready for a good night's sleep.

Our overnight guests will enjoy clean and comfortable sleeping accommodations that include a soft bed, clean blankets and access to fresh water at all times. At bedtime we provide a calming spa-like atmosphere with aromatherapy and soft soothing music to send our canine guests gently to sleep.

To help us make your dog feel more relaxed and at home, please feel free to bring their favorite blanket or non-squeak toy. And you can also bring your dog’s regular food and treats. We’ll accommodate any special dietary needs, and we won't charge you extra for administering medication.

Before your dog's first overnight or extended stay, we do encourage you to bring your pup to our location to get acquainted with our facility and our team.

Policies

All dogs must have current vaccinations as prescribed by your veterinarian. Please bring a copy of your dogs vaccination records on your first visit - we will return them to you. We will also need an emergency telephone number in case we need to contact you.

Cancellation

We're a boutique facility with a limited number of rooms available for our guests. Because of this, we ask that you carefully review our cancellation policy. That said, we understand that plans can change and so we've adopted policies that we think are both straightforward and fair.

Non-holiday Cancellation

For most of the year, you may cancel your reservation with no penalty up to 1 day prior to arrival. If you cancel less than 24 hours before check-in, you will be charged for one night.

Holiday Cancellation

Due to high holiday demand, we require that you cancel no later than 7 days prior to your scheduled check-in. If you cancel less than 7 days prior to check-in, you will be charged $100. If you book your stay within 7 days prior to check-in for a holiday and then subsequently cancel, this will also result in a $100 charge to the credit card on file.
