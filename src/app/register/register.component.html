Things to Know

If this the first time you've considered day care or boarding for your dog, please read the following information about our general requirements. Don't hesitate to give us a call or email us if you have any further questions. We want to you to be confident that our primary goal is to make sure all of the dogs in our care are in a healthy and safe environment.

Vaccinations

DHLPP/Rabies vaccinations are required every three years. Dogs must also have a current immunization for Bordetella (kennel cough).

General Health

Dogs must be in good health and free of any communicable disease for 30 days before coming to Daycare. If your dog becomes sick with a communicable disease, we require veterinarian certification of good health before returning to Daycare.

Dogs must be free of fleas, ticks and worms. Flea collars are not allowed.

If we think that your dog is unwell or has parasites, we will isolate him or her and contact you for immediate pick up.

Age

For group play, dogs must be at least 14 weeks of age and completed all of their puppy shots. We have separate yards for the special attention and feeding of younger puppies and senior dogs in our care.

Spay/Neuter

All dogs six months of age or older must be spayed or neutered.

Ready to Get Started?

Use our online customer registration module to register with The Barkmont. Don't forget your dog's vaccination status - you can upload it to your account or ask your vet to send it directly to us at vax@the-barkmont.com.
