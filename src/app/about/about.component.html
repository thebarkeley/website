Welcome to The Barkmont

The Barkmont provides the finest dog daycare and boarding services in Berkeley and its surrounding communities. We're located at the corner of San Pablo and Pardee in West Berkeley, convenient locally and a few short blocks away from the Ashby and University exits of the I80/580.

Take a virtual tour!

We remodelled the building with a design focused on providing the highest quality dog daycare and boarding services in the Bay Area, a job that included updating the plumbing and electricity, adding floor drains, and installing modern heating and ventilation systems.

Sign Up Today

Interested in signing your dog up for daycare or boarding? It's as simple as 1-2-3:

  1. Start by reviewing our Requirements for vaccinations and general health.
  2. Next, use our online Registration tool to fill out the necessary forms. Don't forget to send us your dog's vaccination status: either upload it to your account or ask your veterinarian to send it to us at vax@the-barkmont.com.
  3. Then book your dog's first stay.

Our Focus

Daycare

A highlight of The Barkmont is our four indoor play yards. Our main yards sit under large skylights that soak the building in warm sunlight throughout the day. Complementing our main yards are smaller adjacent yards for senior dogs, puppies or dogs who do best in smaller play groups.

Our yards are equipped with high-resolution web cams for you to check in on your pup from your computer or smart phone.

Boarding

Our overnight guests enjoy clean and comfortable sleeping accommodations that come with a soft bed, clean blankets and access to fresh water at all times. At bedtime we provide a calming spa-like atmosphere with aromatherapy and soft soothing music to send our canine guests gently to sleep.

Grooming

We have a dog wash station at The Barkmont and offer basic washing, brush and nail clipping as a service. Contact us to learn more.

Training

A key to happiness is being a lifelong learner, for both humans and dogs. If you're interested in training, we can help you find and schedule the right trainer for your dog. Contact us to learn more.

A Team You Can Trust

The Barkmont is staffed 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Our dog care professionals are trained in dog body language and behavior to ensure your dog is always in a safe and healthy environment.
