Our Facility

We're located at the corner of San Pablo and Pardee in West Berkeley, convenient locally and from the Ashby and University exits of the I80/580.

Our building is 4,000 sqft. Constructed in 1938 as a typewriter factory and sales office, the building was more recently used as an auto repair shop. After taking over the building, we undertook a complete remodel of the space, starting with a floor to rafter cleaning and demolition of the interior space. We've given the building a modern design focused on providing the highest quality dog daycare and boarding services for your dog , a job that included updating the plumbing and electricity, adding floor drains, and installing modern heating and ventilation systems.

Our Focus

Given the modest size of our facility, our design is optimized to accommodate small to medium sized dogs. We can also accommodate a limited number of larger dogs. But if you have a large active dog, we may not be the right facility for you.

Our aim is to make the experience for all our dogs the best it can possibly be, so we will keep strict limits on the number of dogs in each of our play areas. Our easy-to-use scheduling software (coming soon) will make it easy for both us and you to plan your daycare or boarding stay without hassle or worry.

Daycare

A highlight of The Barkeley is our four individual play yards. Our main yards sit under large skylights that soak the building in warm sunlight throughout the day. Complementing our main yards are smaller adjacent yards for senior dogs, puppies or dogs who do best in smaller play groups.

And all of our yards are equipped with high-resolution web cams for you to check in on your pup from your computer or smart phone.

Boarding

Another key focus of The Barkeley is to provide the best overnight care in the area for your dog. Our standard kennels can be configured to accommodate one or two dogs, and we will also offer a limited number of larger stall-styled rooms.

Training

We don't have certified dog trainers on staff at The Barkeley. However, we are prepared to work with local trainers who can provide training for your dog during their stay at The Barkeley. Contact us to learn more.

Grooming

We have a brand new dog wash station at The Barkeley and we offer basic washing, brush and nail clipping as a service. For full service grooming (as with training), contact us to learn more about scheduling a spa session with one of our affiliated fully-equipped local groomers while your dog is in our care.
