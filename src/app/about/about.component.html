Our Facility

We're located at the corner of San Pablo and Pardee in West Berkeley, convenient locally and a few short blocks away from the Ashby and University exits of the I80/580.

Our building is 4,000 sqft. Constructed in 1938 as a typewriter factory and sales office, the space was more recently used as an auto repair shop. After taking over the building, we undertook a complete remodel, starting with a floor to rafter cleaning and demolition. We've given the building a modern design focused on providing the highest quality dog daycare and boarding services in the Bay Area, a job that included updating the plumbing and electricity, adding floor drains, and installing modern heating and ventilation systems.

Our Focus

Our aim is to make the experience for all our dogs the best it can possibly be, so we will keep strict limits on the number of dogs in each of our play areas. And for you, we have an easy-to-use scheduling (coming soon) module, so you can plan your daycare or boarding stay with us without hassle or worry.

Given the modest size of our facility, our design is ideal for small to medium sized dogs. We can also care for a limited number of larger dogs. Call us to find out if The Barkeley is a good fit for your pup.

Daycare

A highlight of The Barkeley is our four indoor play yards. Our main yards sit under large skylights that soak the building in warm sunlight throughout the day. Complementing our main yards are smaller adjacent yards for senior dogs, puppies or dogs who do best in smaller play groups.

And all of our yards are equipped with high-resolution web cams for you to check in on your pup from your computer or smart phone.

Boarding

The Barkeley is staffed 24 hours a day, 7 days a week by our professional and caring staff. In addition to around-the-clock care, our overnight guests will enjoy clean and comfortable sleeping accommodations that come with a soft bed, clean blankets and access to fresh water at all times. Our boarding area is climate controlled so guests are always comfortable. At bedtime we provide a calming spa-like atmosphere with aromatherapy and soft soothing music to send our canine guests gently to sleep.

Training

We don't have certified dog trainers on staff at The Barkeley. However, we are prepared to work with local trainers who can provide training for your dog during their stay at The Barkeley. Contact us to learn more.

Grooming

We have a brand new dog wash station at The Barkeley and we offer basic washing, brush and nail clipping as a service. For full service grooming (as with training), contact us to learn more about scheduling a spa session with one of our affiliated fully-equipped local groomers while your dog is in our care.
