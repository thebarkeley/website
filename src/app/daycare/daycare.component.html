Playtime at The Barkeley

At The Barkeley we know that dogs have an instinctual desire to be part of a pack. Giving your pup regular play time with other dogs has a number of benefits. Here are a few that top the list:

  • Increases socialization, both with other dogs and people
  • Reinforces positive behavior in other settings, including and importantly, your own home
  • Promotes physical fitness

The daycare program at The Barkeley is cageless and designed to encourage open play among the dogs in our care. We have over 2,500sf of indoor yards - they are sunny and feature modern climate control and drainage systems. Your dog will have a full day of socializing, safe play, and lounging about with friends at The Barkeley, ready to come home with you in the evening happy and ready to kick back.

We place dogs in play groups based on size and temperament to ensure each dog's health, safety and happiness. And every dog spends their day under the constant supervision of our staff. And we've equipped each yard with a high resolution web cam so you can check in on your pet throughout the day from your computer or smart phone. You'll see firsthand how attentive our yard specialists are with your dog.

Before staying with us, we'll meet with you and your dog to ensure they will be a good fit for open play.

Hours

While our facility is staffed 24 hours a day 365 days a year, to ensure the safety of our staff and overnight guests, please note that our lobby is open only at the times listed below.

Open Mon-Fri: 7am - 7pm, Sat & Sun: 9am - 5pm

Early Drop Off and Late Pickup

Our Lobby is open from 7am-7pm Monday through Friday and 9am-5pm Saturday and Sunday for pick up and drop off. Should you need an early drop off or late pick up outside of our lobby hours, please call ahead of time during our regular business hours to make special arrangements. Please note that there is a $20 fee for all pick ups and drop offs after regular hours. Although we are staffed 24-hours a day and try to accommodate clients whenever we can, there are no late pick ups allowed after 9pm to ensure the safety of our staff and the other dogs in our care.

Cancellation

We ask that you cancel daycare reservations no later than 10am the day before your visit. You will be charged for a day's visit for late cancellations and/or no-shows.
