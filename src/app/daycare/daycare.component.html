Day Care

Supervised Play Yards

Our facility includes four play yards that are fully supervised. We've organized our yards to group dogs by size, personality and play style to make sure your dog is safe and comfortable. Before staying with us, we'll meet with you and your dog to ensure they will be a good fit for open play.

Our play yards have state-of-the-art web cams and so you can check in on your pet throughout the day from your computer or smart phone. You’ll see firsthand how attentive our yard specialists are with your dog.

A Team You Can Trust

The Barkeley's team of dog care professionals will provide a safe and fully supervised environment. Our yard attendants are trained on dog body language and behavior to ensure the safety and well being of the dogs in our care. We're dedicated to giving your dog a day filled with:

  • exercise
  • socialization
  • positive education

Our team will take detailed notes on your dog’s activities, allowing us to easily identify subtle behavioral changes that may indicate possible health issues. Because we're a boutique facility we're confident we'll be abel to give your dog the time and attention they deserve.
