Playtime at The Barkmont

The daycare program at The Barkmont is cageless and designed to encourage open play among the dogs in our care. We have over 2,000sf of indoor yards - they are sunny and feature modern climate control and drainage systems. Your dog will have a full day of socializing, safe play, and lounging about with friends at The Barkmont, ready to come home with you in the evening happy and ready to kick back.

We place dogs in play groups based on size and temperament to ensure each dog's health, safety and happiness. And every dog spends their day under the constant supervision of our staff.

Before staying with us, we'll meet with you and your dog to ensure they will be a good fit for open play.

Monthly Subscriptions

If you would like to use our daycare service on a daily basis, consider purchasing a month-to-month subscription. The cost for a subscription is $585 and entitles you to daycare for your dog any day for the entire month. Subscriptions can be renewed automatically and they are a great option for customers who need regular daycare for their pup while they are at work.

We offer a limited number of subscriptions in order to ensure we have space for all our guests on any given day. Talk to us on your next visit to The Barkmont about enrollment and availability.

Hours

While our facility is staffed 24 hours a day 365 days a year, to ensure the safety of our staff and overnight guests, please note that our lobby is open only at the times listed below.

Open Mon-Fri: 7am - 7pm, Sat & Sun: 9am - 5pm

Early Drop Off and Late Pickup

Our Lobby is open from 7am-7pm Monday through Friday and 9am-5pm Saturday and Sunday for pick up and drop off. Should you need an early drop off or late pick up outside of our lobby hours, please call ahead of time during our regular business hours to make special arrangements. Please note that there is a $25 fee for all pick ups and drop offs after regular hours. Although we are staffed 24-hours a day and try to accommodate clients whenever we can, there are no late pick ups allowed after 9pm to ensure the safety of our staff and the other dogs in our care.
